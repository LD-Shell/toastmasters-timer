How the timer works

The timer changes color to provide visual cues for your speech duration.

  • Green: The minimum time has been met.
  • Yellow: The time is nearing its end. You should begin to wrap up.
  • Red: The maximum time has been exceeded. You should conclude your speech.

The list below keeps track of all speakers and their final times. You can remove an entry if a mistake was made or export the entire list to a PDF.
